Values & Philanthropy

Chi Psi Mission Statement

Chi Psi is a private Fraternity of gentlemen devoted to the personal and collective needs of its members. A selective association of diverse individuals, Chi Psi is dedicated to the cultivation of a unique environment which instills a lifelong commitment to Brotherhood.

The mission of the Chi Psi Fraternity is to create and maintain an enduring society which encourages the sharing of traditions and values, respect for oneself and others, and responsibility to the university and community.

Chi Psi Values

  • Unique Brotherhood that forges among individuals a lasting bond that transcends self-interest and the limitations of time or place.
  • Growth of the individual in a social environment that nurtures integrity, dignity, and maturity while enjoying shared college experiences.
  • Intellectual growth and the attainment of human potential.
  • Personal and collective involvement in serving the needs of the university, the community, and society.
  • Tradition, because it provides stability in a rapidly changing world and develops a sense of personal identity.

Philanthropy

Chi Psi is currently participating in No Shave November for awareness of testicular cancer and men's mental health. So far this month, the brotherhood has donated $250 to the cause.
